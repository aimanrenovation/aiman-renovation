# Prompt Vidéo Hero

## Paramètres Gemini
- Vidéo
- 16:9
- x2 qualité
- Veo 3.1 quality

## Prompt

```
Fixed camera with very subtle slow zoom in throughout the entire video. 8 seconds total. No cuts, single continuous shot.

A traditional Alsatian half-timbered house (colombages) seen from the front garden. Time-lapse style transformation from complete ruin to beautifully renovated home.

Second 0-1: The house is a complete ruin. Cracked timber frame, crumbling white plaster falling between dark beams, broken clay roof tiles with holes, rotting wooden shutters hanging off hinges, overgrown garden with tall weeds, gray overcast sky. Everything is still and desolate.

Second 1-2: Scaffolding rapidly assembles around the house. Five workers in red polo shirts and black pants arrive carrying tools. One begins scraping old plaster off the wall with a trowel. Movement begins.

Second 2-3.5: Fast-forward action. Workers move rapidly. One on the roof removing old tiles, one on scaffolding stripping the facade, one in the garden pulling weeds. Old plaster and debris fall. The house looks stripped down to its bones. The sky starts to lighten.

Second 3.5-5: Transformation visible. Workers apply insulation panels to the facade. New roof tiles being laid one by one. Fresh white lime render being spread between dark timber beams. The garden is cleared. Colors become warmer.

Second 5-6.5: Finishing touches. One worker painting the timber beams fresh dark brown. Another installing new wooden shutters. Garden being landscaped with gravel path and green plants. Windows being cleaned to sparkle.

Second 6.5-8: Final reveal. The house is completely renovated: pristine white render between perfectly maintained dark timber beams, new clay roof tiles, freshly painted shutters, flowering garden with stone path, warm golden hour sunlight illuminating everything. Workers stand at a distance admiring their work. Beautiful and inviting.

No recognizable faces visible, workers always seen from behind or side at medium distance. Bright natural daylight transitioning to warm golden hour. Alsace France village setting with rolling hills in background. Cinematic quality.
```

## Prompt version mobile (portrait 9:16)

```
Same scene as above but framed in portrait 9:16 aspect ratio. Camera closer to the house, showing mainly the facade and entrance. Same time-lapse transformation from ruin to renovated. Same workers in red polo shirts. Same timing. No faces visible. Alsatian half-timbered house. 8 seconds.
```
